First select your grid from dropdown, then click on any box you like, the aim of the game is to select all correct pair of boxes with minimum mistakes. 1 point is for correct pairing, and -0.3 for wrong pairing, you can click on the boxes to see the number in it. Pairing happens when two boxes are selected with same number. After selecting grid type, click on the boxes to play the game
